About Us

Greenland Particle Boards Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated in the year 2010 with the objective of setting up a ultra-modern particle board manufacturing facility with a capacity of 180 CBM at Perumbavoor in the state of Kerala. The plant was commissioned in the year 2014 and the commercial production started from June 2015.The Company has a capacity of laminating 5000 boards per day and carries an inventory of more than 250 plus decors. Our clients are spread all over India as well as in couple of other countries. We employ 240 people, we provide in house accommodation and food for the entire work force. We are using virgin plantation rubber for manufacturing high quality particle board which is a renewable resource.
